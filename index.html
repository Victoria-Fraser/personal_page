A message to my love

I created this page for you because I wanted to do something special for you, something you would love and appreciate. This is my virtual love letter to you.

This past year with you has been nothing short from amazing. I found a love with you that is so easy and fun. It's a new feeling to be so relaxed and happy with someone. I'm glad I found that in you. You've shown me love and kindness, you've made me laugh and roll my eyes at you more times than I (or you) can count, and I find that I keep loving you more and more.

You're smart and you inspire me because there is nothing you won't try learn. I remember when I first met you (again) I was so in awe of how much you could do, but now a year later I respect and admire you for it. I've seen where it's brought you and I'm so proud of you. I'm excited to see what this year has in store for you. I'm sure there'll be some easy and tough times, but I'm here to go through it all with you, and I've got your back always.

You're funny and you're weird. You make me laugh even when I don't want to. You're also silly and cute sometimes. And what I really love is that I can be all those things with you too. You've allowed me to feel comfortable and at home when I'm around you. I really appreciate that. And I will follow you anywhere.

My heart is happy with you. I'm looking forward to many more adventures with you.

I love you with all my heart.
